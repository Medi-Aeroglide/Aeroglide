The Problem

  • Slow Delivery: Manual transport in hospitals is inefficient during emergencies.
  • Human Error: Misplacement of lab samples or medicines.
  • Resource Waste: Valuable medical staff time spent walking between wings.

AeroGlide Solution

  • Autonomous Drones: Self-navigating aerial units for point-to-point transfer.
  • High Accuracy: RFID tagging ensures correct delivery every time.
  • 24/7 Operation: Always ready for emergency supply runs.
